Until recently, our server has been hosted through Minecraft Realms. However, the time has come to upgrade to dedicated hosting through Server.pro. For players this means full modpack and plugin support! The potential for fun has grown exponentially.

Logging on

Connecting is simple.
  1. Start by copying the IP adress [51.79.76.104:40461] to your clipboard.
  2. Next, navigate to the Minecraft splash screen (Where it says single player, multiplayer, etc.) and select Multiplayer.
  3. Finally, click "Add Server" and paste the IP adress in the "Adress" field.
Previous players will be added to the whitelist by default. However, new players will need to be recommended in order to be added.
